About

Virtual Reality (VR) is on the cusp of blowing up in today's society. People are becoming increasingly aware of VR headsets from the humble Google Cardboard to the big names like HTC Vive and Oculus Rift.


VR is mostly known for its gaming applications. After all, who wouldn't want to be fully immersed while destroying zombies? However, VR holds potential for much more than simply entertainment. It can also be used in business, education, healthcare, and defense, among other applications.


As members of the Virtual Reality Society (VRS) here at UT Dallas, we are excited about all that VR has to offer, and strive to share our enthusiasm with the UT Dallas and local communities through talks, demos, workshops, and outreach events.


We certainly hope that you're as excited as we are to get immersed in the world of Virtual Reality. Subscribe to our mailing list to stay in the loop about our events, and we'll see you there.
